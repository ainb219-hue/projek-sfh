Application of Derivatives in Bioscience (SFH)

Scenario: Broiler Chicken Growth Rate (Malaysian Smart-Poultry Farm)

In Malaysia, heat stress affects how chickens grow. This graph tracks the 42-day cycle to optimize feed and harvest timing.

Mathematical Model

Growth Function:

f(x) = -0.04x³ + 2.8x² + 5x + 45

Derivative (Growth Rate per day):

f'(x) = -0.12x² + 5.6x + 5
